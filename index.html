About Us

The Kentucky Entrepreneur Hall of Fame is a physical and virtual destination that shares and celebrates the stories of Kentucky's most successful entrepreneurs.

Our mission is to raise awareness of the impact that entrepreneurship has made in the Commonwealth and encourage others to pursue similar ambitious endeavors.

Nominations are open for the current year's class. To nominate, fill out this form. Anyone can nominate.

If you have questions or would like to become involved please email us at ky@entrepreneurhof.com.
