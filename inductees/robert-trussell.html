Robert B. Trussell

Tempur Sealy International, Inc.
Trussell is the former president of Tempur Sealy International. After convincing the Swedish creators of Tempur Pedic to allow him to sell their mattresses and pillows in the U.S., Trussell started his company by selling the first mattresses out of his car, haggling with Brookstone's merchandising reps and mailing conditional free samples to chiropractors.
