Inductee

Jess Correll

First Southern National Bank
Jesse (Jess) T. Correll is founder and chairman of the board for First Southern Bancorp, Inc. First Southern is based in Stanford, Kentucky and is the parent company of First Southern National Bank. Correll, 62, is an entrepreneur at heart and a creative, visionary leader. He has a passion for giving, which led to the establishment of The River Foundation, a private foundation that gives to Christ-centered organizations, both locally and throughout the world. Jess serves as President and Chairman of the Board for this Foundation.
