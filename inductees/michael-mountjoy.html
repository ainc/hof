Inductee

Michael B. Mountjoy

MCM CPAs and Advisors
Mike is a Founding Partner of MCM CPAs & Advisors, a Firm that has grown to be the largest CPA firm headquartered in Kentucky. MCM now has six offices throughout Kentucky, Indiana and Ohio. Mike has been active in the accounting world at both the state and national level, serving as President of the Kentucky Society of CPAs and serving on the board of the American Institute of CPAs. Mike has served in numerous local leadership roles throughout his career. He is the Former Chair of the Louisville Chamber of Commerce and The Community Foundation. Currently, he serves as Chair of the Gheens Foundation and as Trustee at the University of the Cumberlands. Mike is an active investor in several start-up ventures.
