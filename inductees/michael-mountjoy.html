Inductee

Michael B. Mountjoy

MCM CPA and Advisors
Mike founded the firm in 1980 that ultimately became MCM. The firms original goal was to grow to a size that could service the growing entrepreneurial businesses in Kentucky. Through the years the firm has grown to be the largest cpa firm headquatered in Kentucky with offices in Kentucky, Ohio and Indiana. While managing the firm, Mike has been active in the profession at the state and national level he served as the president of the KY Sociey of CPA's and on the Board of the American Institute of CPAs. Mike has served in a leadership roles in many local organizations. He is a past chair of the Louisville Chamber of Commerce and the Commuity Foundation. He is the Chair of the Gheens Foundation and serves as a trustee at the University of the Cumberlands. He is an active investor in a number of start up ventures. He has invested in commercial real estate, the restaurant industry, and is a co-founder of Soccer Holdings, the owner of Louiville's mens and womens professional soccer teams. He has managed his family's farming, timber and coal activities. Mike is married to Carolyn Mountjoy. He is the father of 4 and grandfather of 12.
